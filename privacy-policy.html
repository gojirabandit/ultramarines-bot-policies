⚔️ Privacy Policy - Ultramarines Bot

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Ultramarines Bot ("the Bot"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Discord bot. By using the Bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot collects and processes the following information:

  • User IDs: Discord user IDs to identify who is using commands
  • Server IDs: Discord server (guild) IDs where the Bot is installed
  • Message Content: Only messages sent directly to the Bot via commands or DMs
  • Role Information: Role names and IDs when using the DM feature
  • Command Usage: Records of which commands are used for functionality purposes
What We Do NOT Collect:
  • We do not read or store messages in channels unless directly sent to the Bot
  • We do not collect personal information like emails, phone numbers, or real names
  • We do not track your browsing or activity outside of Bot interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide Bot functionality (commands, DM relays, AI responses)
  • Relay DMs to designated channels when users message the Bot
  • Generate AI-powered responses to user questions
  • Improve Bot performance and fix bugs

4. AI Processing

When you use the /counsel_the_primarch command, your question is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a roleplay response. This data is processed according to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • The Bot operates in-memory and does not maintain a permanent database
  • DM relay messages are sent immediately and not stored
  • Command logs may be temporarily stored for debugging purposes
  • No long-term data retention occurs

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties except:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): For AI-powered responses
  • Discord: As required for Bot functionality through Discord's API
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the Bot at any time
  • Request the Bot be removed from your server
  • Disable DMs to the Bot to prevent message relay
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data processed by the Bot. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Bot developer through Discord or the support server.
