⚔️ Features - Ultramarines Bot

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

The Ultramarines Bot brings the glory of the XIII Legion to your Discord server. Below is a complete list of features and commands available to all warriors of Ultramar.

1. Primarch Communication

Commands that channel the wisdom and authority of Roboute Guilliman himself.

/primarch_wisdom

Receive wisdom from the Lord of Ultramar. The Primarch shares tactical insights and motivational guidance.

/guilliman_judges

Present a tactical dilemma or situation for the Primarch's judgment. He will evaluate your scenario and provide his ruling.

/codex_quote

Receive a quote from the sacred Codex Astartes to guide your actions.

/take_the_oath

Swear an Oath of Moment before the Primarch and your battle-brothers. Your oath will be recorded until fulfilled.

2. Combat Records System

Track your warrior's achievements, battles, and honors in the Chapter's archives.

/create_combat_record

Establish your warrior's combat record in the Chapter archives. Set your lore name, company, rank, and specialization.

/view_combat_record

View combat records for yourself or another warrior. Displays all statistics, battle honors, and current status.

/edit_combat_record

Update your combat record details such as lore name, company, rank, or specialization.

/delete_combat_record

Permanently remove your combat record from the Chapter archives. This action cannot be undone.

/combat_record_leaderboard

View the top warriors ranked by various categories: total events, wins, commendations, or win rate.

3. Event Logging

Submit and track participation in Chapter events, battles, and activities.

/log_event

Submit an event for approval. Includes event name, type (PvP, PvE, Training, etc.), host, result, screenshot, and optional notes. Events require approval before being recorded.

/edit_event

Request modifications to a previously submitted event. Edits require approval.

4. Commendations & Penance

Recognize exceptional service or assign penance for infractions.

/commend_warrior

Award a commendation to a fellow warrior for exceptional service. Commendations are tracked and can unlock special roles at 50 and 100 commendations.

/assign_penanceADMIN

Assign penance to a warrior for infractions against the Codex Astartes. Set duration in hours, days, or weeks.

/remove_penanceADMIN

Lift active penance from a warrior before its natural expiration.

5. Weekly Attendance Tracking

Monitor weekly participation with automatic tracking and reporting.

/weekly_stats

View your weekly event attendance breakdown by day, or check another warrior's attendance.

/weekly_leaderboard

View the top warriors ranked by weekly attendance.

/force_weekly_resetADMIN

Manually trigger the weekly reset and generate the weekly report.

6. Administrative Commands

Commands reserved for Chapter leadership and bot administrators.

/dm_rolesADMIN

Issue a directive to warriors via DM based on their roles. Select up to 5 roles to message simultaneously.

/speak_as_primarchADMIN

Send a message to any channel as the Primarch himself.

/primarch_directiveADMIN

Issue a formal directive with rich formatting. Supports custom titles, multi-line messages, images, thumbnails, and up to 6 document link buttons.

/reset_combat_recordsADMIN

Modify warrior combat records. Add or subtract events, wins, losses, or commendations.

7. Automatic Features

Features that run automatically without command input.

  • DM Relay: Direct messages sent to the bot are relayed to a designated channel for review
  • Daily Battle Reports: Automated daily summaries of Chapter activity
  • Weekly Summary Reports: Comprehensive weekly attendance and activity reports
  • Automatic Weekly Reset: Weekly attendance tracking resets automatically each week
  • GitHub Storage: All combat records and oaths are persistently stored with automatic sharding for scalability
  • Role Rewards: Automatic role assignment at commendation milestones (50 and 100)

8. Data Storage

The bot uses GitHub-based persistent storage with automatic sharding to handle large amounts of data efficiently. Combat records, oaths of moment, and weekly tracking data are all securely stored and backed up.
